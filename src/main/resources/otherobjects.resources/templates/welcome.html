A quick guide to using the workbench

  • Use the Navigator and right click to add/modify folders.
  • In the Listing. Click to select. Double-click to edit.
  • The Preview panel will always preview the currently selected item.

New and noteworthy

Security

  • Support for administrators (with full access) and guests (who can only browse the site).
  • Passwords are stored securely and your login details can be remembered.
  • Usernames do not have to be email addresses although it is strongly recommended that they are and this will probably be enforced for most sites.
